COLLEGE OF CRIMINAL JUSTICE

College of Criminal Justice offers undergraduate degree programs in criminal justice, criminology, forensic science, law enforcement administration, and homeland security. It prepares students for careers in law enforcement, corrections, and the legal system. It provides a comprehensive education in criminal justice, emphasizing critical thinking, ethical decision-making, and social responsibility. Graduates are well-equipped to contribute to public safety and justice.
