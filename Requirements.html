Freshman Requirements

TAGUIG CITY UNIVERSITY FRESHMEN'S REQUIREMENTS

1. Grade 11 Rating Card and Grade 12 1st Semester Rating Card duly signed by HS Principal
2. Certificate of Good Moral Character
3. PSA/NSO Birth Certificate
4. One pc. 2x2 ID Picture (white background with name tag)
5. COMELEC certification of parent (below 18 years old)
6. COMELEC certification of Student (18) years old & above issued after May 15, 2019)
7. Learner Reference Number (LRN)
8. Admission Requirements Form (ARF)
