FRESHMEN'S ADMISSION

  • Step 1: After passing the examination.
  • Step 2: Get Physical Examination Form at the Medical Dental Services Unit (MDSU), main building. Medical and Dental Exam can be done at any Barangay Health Center.
    -CBC (Blood Count)
    -Chest X-ray
    -Fecalysis (stool)
    –Urinalysis (urine)
    –Dental
  • Step:3 Submit Medical and Dental Exam Results to Medical Dental Service Unit (MDSU), main building and get a medical certification.
  • Step:4 Go to Registrar office (on the given date) and submit all the original documents for evaluation.
    • – Birth Certificate
    – Good Moral Character from High School
    – Barangay Clearance
    – Voter’s ID (Photocopy) Bring original for verification
    – High School Form 137 and 138
